## First 5 Things To Do

1. **Call the inner circle** (contacts above)
2. **Secure the home, pets, and kids**
3. **Find the will** (location listed above)
4. **Do NOT close or cancel anything yet** — just make sure this month's bills get paid
5. **Read [first-72-hours.md](first-72-hours.md)** for the full step-by-step guide

---

## First 5 Things NOT To Do

1. **Do not pay debts from your own money** — the estate pays, not you
2. **Do not notify the bank** until you've secured cash for immediate needs (they may freeze the account)
3. **Do not publish the home address** in the obituary (burglary risk)
4. **Do not throw away mail** for at least a year
5. **Do not make major life decisions** for at least 30 days
